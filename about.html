About Royal Food Cafe

Here at Royal Food Cafe, we believe that food is a celebration of life and that traditional foods are an enduring part of our culture. Family owned and operated, we challenge ourselves everyday to live up to our name by preparing and serving traditional, Indian home style meals with devotion, elegance, and grace. Each of us has special memories of home-made-food - the kind that keeps us longing for it when we are away from home. Capturing this authentic flavor has become a critical undertaking for Royal Food Cafe and we are proud to continue to cook traditional meals from recipes perfected from over thousands of years.
